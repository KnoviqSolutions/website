Services



Data Strategy

Before addressing your data needs, it is imperative to understand your goals and objectives. Subsequently, we focus on using data strategically to solve your business problems. Knoviq Solutions understands how your data strategy can help to improve your aquisition, storage, management, sharing, and use of data.

Strategy Review

Understanding where you're headed and what your current plan of action is important in identifying the obstacles and the current challenges you are facing. Within your initial review, we will highlight your current data operations and capabilities to better help position you for positive outcomes.

Scalability Review

In order to help growing businesses scale up, we will take a deep dive into what current implementations work best for your environment, while keeping your goals as number one. We will tailor this to fit the needs of the business t so you'll be capable of maintaining the work once it is done.

Execution Plan

We understand your vision is just the start. We'll design a custom plan to best execute this vision and bring it to life to make your data work for you. Your execution plan will be built to address the business goals, problems, outcomes, areas of improvement and the management of your data for future use.

Data Management & Integration

Most businesses have multiple data sources from various systems. This could result in mismanagement of your data. Knoviq Solutions imports and manages your data in one central location. Having your data managed and integrated centrally allows our dashboards to deliver consistent and reliable insights.

Eliminate Data Silos

Seamlessly bringing your data together into one centralized location helps provide a complete story to your business problems. Knoviq Solutions integrates your most common platforms to allow clients to have more reliable and up to date information when making critical decisions.

Custom Database

We understand the starting point for your business is the important need to have a reliable, up-to-date database to store your data. Knoviq Solutions will clean, set-up, and manage your database within a cloud computing environment for your business's security and scalability.

Maintenance & Support

It can be challenging for growing businesses to manage and intergrate their data within one secure location. We consider this challenge a mission for us to partner with you to provide ongoing support and maintance for your data storage, while you can focus on your business's success.

Reporting

Our reporting is crafted to give you automated, custom, and timely analysis of your business's performance on demand.

Our data analysis is powered by using R and python, both which are open source softwares, that allows you to visualize your data in various chart types and custom view for each type of data and market.

Automating your reporting allows for you to have access to text analytics, churn analysis, financial analysis, operating data, and more on a frequent and reliable schedule and when you need it most.

Most importantly, Knoviq Solutions enables multiple channels to share your reporting with your teammates so you can all be empowered to make data driven decisions.

Dashboards

Our dashboards are powered by SQL, python and R to help automate your metrics. Our concise reporting is intended to keep you focused on the details that matter the most to your business. We've built them so that your team can interact and ask more meaningful questions to help solve your business problems.
